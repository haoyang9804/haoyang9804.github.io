About Me

I'm a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST, supervised by Prof. Shing-Chi Cheung. I'm now a member of the CASTLE Group. My research topic mainly fouses on Compiler Fuzzing. I have also participated in research works about Bug Isolation. I am also interested in Compiler Verification and Compiler Translation Validation. Besides research, I love coding, such as designing and implementing a compiler or verifying a simple type system.

Before I joined in CASTLE Group, I did research on compiler testing under the guidance of Prof. Junjie Chen for more than one year when I was an undergraduate at Tianjin University.
